About Us

Agro International is a global sourcing company specializing in the import of Raw Cashew Nuts from African countries such as Ghana, Benin, and Ivory Coast. We supply these premium quality nuts to India's leading processing industries. Our deep-rooted relationships with farm-level aggregators and streamlined logistics ensure reliable, timely deliveries every time.
